About TISS TECH

Technology, Information, Systems and Services.

1.   The Company

    The TISS was established in 2002 to fulfill the need of the domestic market in the formation of a new management model based on Information Technology.

    This new model should be able to act intensively on the market with the objective of generating fast and lasting changes.

   With a merged composite of engineers, administrators and IT specialists, the team which was formed at TISS began to act jointly in 1995, on specific projects of public urban transportation system in the metropolitan region of Recife - Pernambuco, Alagoas and Rio Grande do Norte.

    The increase of demands to the team led to the formation of a company, the TissTech. The involved team used the multidisciplinary expertise to develop a work methodology turned toward the integration of technologies and solutions in order to remedy deficiencies and satisfy needs.

2.   Organization

   As a modern vision company, we organize our activities in a structure which ensures quality while seeking to avoid spending excesses. Each project, service or product is agglutinated into a business core. This mixed structure between a classic model and a matrix network, ensures great flexibility, agility and functionality in managing our activities with clients and partners.

OLHO VIVO

It is an innovative business management web system, in which the organization processes are authorized and controlled through a customizable flow.

With easiness of access from anywhere in the world via Internet, having only a password, and the decisions support tools, the administrator day-to-day routine becomes more peaceful, gaining free time to devote to other activities.

The customization brings a unique differential into the market: the system's ability to fully adapt to the needs of the companies.

The system is based on the following foundations:

  • Customization: The Company does not need to change its way of working, the system adapts to the reality of the company;
  • Workflow: Sequence of steps needed to achieve the automation of business process;
  • Process of collecting, organizing, analyzing, sharing and monitoring information that offer support to the business management;
  • Composed by processes authentication, authorization, and auditing;
  • Dashboard: Illustrated representation of business performance throughout all the organization and enabling approval of processes.

It has the following modules:

  • Documents;
  • Financial;
  • Human Resources (HR);
  • Processes;
  • Accounting;
  • Patrimony;
  • Stock;
  • Basic Register;
  • Agenda;
  • Business Intelligence.

OTIBUS

The system simulates the effects of adjustments/corrections of bus lines programming, assembling the programming in Graphic mode.

ENERGI

The ENERGI was developed with the purpose of managing endeavors and works in the electricity sector.

Through the system the concessionaire agent generates the XML file, which must be sent monthly to ANEEL (Brazilian Electricity Regulatory Agency), containing the works landmarks and all the technical data of the endeavors. The system stores the generated XML file in case of any future problems.

This tool is timesaving, because it is not necessary to manually fill out the XML file, or redo the basic register or the schedules of landmarks previously achieved, reusing any information previously provided to the system.

The ENERGI system has the following modules:

  • Endeavors;
  • Equipment Module;
  • Transmission Line;
  • Maneuver;
  • General Register;
  • Generating XML;
  • Reports;
  • Security.
WKT

Management system designed to facilitate the processes of the exportation activity, it provides the monitoring of goods from its origin until its final destination, allowing full control of the purchase, transport, legalization of the process and final clearance.

Making the exportation process and the clearance secure and transparent.

Since it is developed in a Web platform, it becomes an easy access and flexible tool and it can be accessed from any device connected to the Internet.
